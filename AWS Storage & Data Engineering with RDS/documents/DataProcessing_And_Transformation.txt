### Part 4: Data Processing & Transformation

Step 1: Read data from S3  
I loaded the raw dataset cleaned_ecommerce_data.csv from the raw/ folder of the S3 bucket nafisa-s3-project-2026 using Python and Boto3.

Step 2: Basic Cleaning  
I checked for missing values and handled them by filling numeric columns with their mean and categorical columns with their mode. I also verified and corrected data types where necessary.

Step 3: Summary Metrics  
Generated descriptive statistics of the dataset to understand the overall distribution of the data.

Step 4: Group-wise Aggregations  
Performed aggregations based on a category column to analyze metrics per group.

Step 5: Store Processed Data Back to S3  
Saved the cleaned and transformed dataset as processed_data.csv in the processed/ folder of the same S3 bucket.
